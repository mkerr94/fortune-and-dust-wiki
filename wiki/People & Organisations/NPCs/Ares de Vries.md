# Overview

The Players see Ares leading a training session when they are first brought inside Fortuna in [[Session 30]].

In [[Session 31]], Ares is found hanged in the Fortuna armoury. His body is brought to [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]]. Although seemingly self-inflicted, Josias believes he was murdered. His body bears suspicious  bruising on the arms and scratch marks on his neck alongside the tell-tale signs of death by hanging.

The Players learn from Josias that Ares was not a well-liked figure in camp. He was involved in an incident a few weeks prior to the Players arrival by which he and his fellow soldier [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint]] were lost in the Dust-dweller’s cavern. Only Ares emerged alive.

While Ares, with the backing of his Captain, protested that there was nothing he could do for Mackie, the incident caused him to lose the trust of his comrades.

In [[Session 38]], the Players learn the truth of the circumstances surrounding Ares' death from [[Silas Seer]]

# Description

Tall, muscular human. He has icy blue eyes and braided white hair.

# Roles

Soldier of [[The Protectorate]], posted at [[Fortuna Outpost]] under the command of [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]].
