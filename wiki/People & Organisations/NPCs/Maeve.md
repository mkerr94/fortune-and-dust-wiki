# Overview

In Faerun, Maeve was tasked with violently conquering surface settlements by Lolth. Amotti ran from her for this reason, refusing to take part.

In [[Session 34]], Amotti reveals to [[wiki/People & Organisations/Player Characters/Caeldrid Àrmann an Gealach|Caeldrid]] that he and Maeve were once part of a powerful Drow house in the Underdark. House Xai. When their house warred with the surface and Maeve slaughtered indiscriminately, Amotti made a blood sacrifice to Lolth to protect innocent lives and destroy the forces of his own house. Maeve has been crazed with a desire for revenge and sought to kill him ever since.

In [[Session 36]], a phantom of Maeve emerges from the body of [[Marius Flint]]. She is revealed to have been hiding in plain sight at [[Fortuna Outpost]]. The infection spreading among the camp was her influence, her control. Maeve reveals to Amotti that she has been granted power by Lolth herself and has come to finally deliver his reckoning. She also reveals knowledge of [[Gambit]], and indicates that he too is embroiled in she and Lolth's web.
