# Overview

Encountered by the Players as they arrived at Fortuna Output during [[Session 30]]. He orders them imprisoned after accusing them of being infected with a great sickness.

# Description

Josias has is a lean, human man in his fifties, with cropped grey hair and dark brown eyes. He has hooped piercings in gold going up his right ear. He is armed with a short-sword and has reinforced shoulder pads emblazoned with the Watchful Sword and the Founder's Wheel.

Josias speaks softly and smoothly, his parlance peppered with words from the Alcadeñan tongue.

# Roles

Captain of [[Fortuna Outpost]].
