# Overview

Communications officer of [[Fortuna Outpost]].

A bone-whistle belonging to Hermus is retrieved by [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]] from Fortuna's well, after seemingly falling in and being lost. It makes no sound, and [[Marius Flint]] explains to Lythara that Hermus uses it in his care of the [[chiropterrings]].

# Description

Hermus is a thin, sinewy man in his late fifties. He wears thin-framed spectacles, the supports of which are pushed back into his wiry grey hair.

# Roles

Charged with the care of the outpost's colony of chiropterrings at the dovecot.
