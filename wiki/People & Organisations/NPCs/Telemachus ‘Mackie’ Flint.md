# Overview

He was killed in action under mysterious circumstances during a raid of the [[The Dust-dwellers|Dust-dweller]]’s cavern a few weeks prior to the Player’s arrival in Fortuna. Also involved was [[Ares de Vries]], who claimed that Mackie was killed while trying to save Ares from the dust-dwellers and that his death was unavoidable.

In [[Session 31]], Captain Josias confirms Mackie to be the owner of [[The Soldier's Locket]] found by [[Sel]].

In [[Session 38]], the ghost of Telemachus appears through the strange power of the time distortion to defend his brother from the remnants of [[Maeve]]'s power. He delivers a final message to [[Marius Flint|Marius]] before fading into blissful oblivion.

> *"I don't know what's keeping me here, little brother, I just know I don't have long. I'm so sorry for leaving you, Marius. You mustn't blame yourself for any of this. You resisted in the end, that's all that matters. It seems you have found your strength after all. Penny and I will always be with you, you know that. In the breeze that carries the dust across the plains, the warmth of the afternoon sun, we'll be there. There's no pain, Marius, you see? Not anymore. This life is merely the river, what comes next is the sea."*

# Description

Mackie was a respected figure among the soldiers of Fortuna.

# Roles

Former soldier of [[The Protectorate]], posted at [[Fortuna Outpost]] under the command of [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]].

# Relationships
