# Overview

Anvil works the Fortuna armoury, and was the first to discover the hanged body of [[Ares de Vries]].

Anvil speaks to [[Wild Bill]] and Gambit during [[Session 32]], where he reveals himself to have a straight-forward, matter-of-fact personality and work ethic. He tells the duo that he was no closer to Ares than anyone else, and surmises that the guilt over the loss of [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint|Telemachus Flint]] must have driven him to take his own life.

# Description

Anvil is a stout dwarf of frizzy black-grey hair and beard. His right eye is missing, having been scorched out. When at work, he wears a heavy leather apron, an iron face-shield and large blacksmith's gloves. Despite the gloves, his hands remain covered in soot underneath.

# Roles

Armourer of [[Fortuna Outpost]].
