# Overview

Encountered by [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]] during her attempted seduction by [[Silas Seer]] during [[Session 35]].

# Description

Ladle is a rotund halfling, with a protruding belly and a scraggly beard. His hair is tied behind his ears underneath a bandana.

# Roles

Ladle serves in the [[Fortuna Outpost]] camp’s kitchens as the cook.
