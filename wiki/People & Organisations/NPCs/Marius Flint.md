# Overview

During [[Session 31]], the Players learn that Marius is the younger brother of the recently deceased [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint]], who died in potentially suspicious circumstances while raiding the [[The Dust-dwellers|Dust-dwellers]] cavern.

The Players meet Marius in [[Session 33]]. They recognise Marius as [[The Young Soldier]] who arrived in [[Clement's Hollow]] and collapsed on the threshold of the Town Hall.

In [[Session 38]], Marius suggests to the Players that he is the source of the infection in [[Fortuna Outpost]] (the corrupting power of [[Maeve]]) and that this curse was inflicted upon him when Maeve visited him in the night - drawn by his feelings of grief and guilt.

# Description

[[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]] describes Marius as ‘the boy’ and labels him ‘sharp, but brittle’ - just like his namesake. He is a slight, short lad in his early 20's. He has a light beard and locks of rough, curly brown hair and bright green eyes. 

# Roles

Soldier of [[The Protectorate]], posted at [[Fortuna Outpost]] under the command of [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]]. 

# Relationships

Younger brother to [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint]], older brother to [[Penny Flint]].
