# Overview

Silas watched over the Players while they are imprisoned in the Fortuna lock-up in [[Session 31]] along with his comrade, [[Braskin Dropstone|Braskin]] the Goliath.

Silas shows an interest in conversation with [[Elias Blackwood|Eli]] and [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]]. Through this, they learn that he was born on [[Orsobia]] - the island prison colony in the Harbour of the Moon in [[Portumluna]] bay. His father was a guard there, and his mother a weaver from the city. Silas also reveals that he possesses certain innate magical abilities, namely what he refers to as the Sight.

In [[Session 38]], while incarcerated in the Fortuna lock-up, Silas reveals to the Players the truth of the events leading to the death of [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint|Telemachus]], [[Ares de Vries|Ares]] and [[Braskin Dropstone|Braskin]].

# Description

Lilac-skinned Tiefling. Silas has bright red eyes and long, curved scimitar. He is a smooth talker and displays a keen intelligence.

# Roles

Soldier of [[The Protectorate]], posted at [[Fortuna Outpost]] under the command of [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]].
