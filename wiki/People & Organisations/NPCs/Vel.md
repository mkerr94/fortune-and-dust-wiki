# Overview

Aged and wizened Drow of the Underdark, and former mentor and confidant of [[Amotti]].

In [[Session 33]], Amotti meets Vel in a strange cavern on the plains outside of [[Fortuna Outpost]]. He is poring over a large, ancient book and delivers a cryptic warning to Amotti.

> *"The light is fading, Amotti. The balance is being tipped. Only in twilight can our people have a future. The dusk at the parting of the day and the blooming of the night. The lines have already been drawn, and the time approaches. Moonlight guide you, young one."*

Afterward, blood pools in Vel's chest and he dies in Amotti's arms.

# Description

Vel wears a rough, hooded robe of brown cloth.
