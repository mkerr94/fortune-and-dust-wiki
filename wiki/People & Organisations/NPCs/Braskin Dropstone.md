# Overview

Braskin guards the players along with [[Silas Seer]] during their incarceration in the Fortuna lock-up in [[Session 31]]. He is a stoic figure of few words.

In [[Session 38]], Braskin is killed while defending Silas from spider-like creatures summoned by [[Maeve]].

# Description

Grey-blue skinned Goliath. He has a shaved head and bears tribal tattoo marks on his skin. He wields a double-sided battle-axe.

# Roles

Soldier of [[The Protectorate]], posted at [[Fortuna Outpost]] under the command of [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu]].
