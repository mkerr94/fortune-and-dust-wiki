# Overview

Aaracokra marksman of Fortuna Outpost. 

In [[Session 31]], [[Silas Seer]] warns the Players that Skylark is equipped with arrows made of [[Dimeritium]] alloy, and that he rarely misses his mark.

When asked about the death of [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint|Telemachus Flint]] by [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]], [[wiki/People & Organisations/Player Characters/Caeldrid Àrmann an Gealach|Caeldrid]] and [[Elias Blackwood|Eli]] during [[Session 32]], Skylark remarks:

> *"All I'll say is this. Mackie was Ares' sworn brother. If it were me, I'd have died before leaving that hell-hole without him."*

During the same conversation, he also displays concern for the welfare of [[Marius Flint]] after the loss of his brother.

# Description

Skylark has a sharp, hooked beak, grey-white feathers and piecing dark eyes. He is lithe and sinewy, and carries a wooden longbow and quiver on his back between folded, muscular wings.

Skylark has a grim demeanour, and speaks in hushed and serious tones.

# Roles

Marksman stationed at [[Fortuna Outpost]].
