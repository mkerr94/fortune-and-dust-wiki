# Overview

Firbolg stablemaster and veterinarian of the [[Fortuna Outpost]].

With the bulk of the forces at Fortuna otherwise engaged, Equinus acts as de facto camp medic for the remaining garrison.

He was the first to inspect the body of [[Ares de Vries]] after he was found hanged in the armoury.

In [[Session 32]], Equinus inspects and clears the Players of inspection before they join the ranks of Fortuna.

# Description

Equinus is a tall, burly Firbolg with a square, sturdy jaw and reddish-brown fur. The surface of his hands are rough and coarse.

# Roles

Stablemaster and veterinarian of the Fortuna Outpost
