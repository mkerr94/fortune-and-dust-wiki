# Overview

[[Marismente|Marismentan]] militia believed to be behind the kidnap of [[Eva Vanderbilt]]. They and others like them have been systematically targeting infrastructure important to the industrial revolution in [[Alcadeña]] (Fortune and Dust: Prologue).

The Players were sent to [[Clement's Hollow]] on the trail of this group as it was the location of their last sighting by the [[The Protectorate|Protectorate]]. It was believed that they may attack the [[The Barnett-Blake Ironworks|Barnett-Blake Ironworks]].

After the Players recover [[Jebediah ‘Jeb’ Clayborn|Jebediah Clayborn]] from the [[Shrine of the Forest]] in [[Session 18]], Jeb recounts that he saw a strange group of Marismente in the Shrine and believes that they rescued him and placed him there for healing. He recalls that there was a strange girl with them, who he identifies as same girl in Eva's pictogram from the [[Vanderbilt Contract]]. They placed a tiara of blue sea-glass on her head in a mysterious ritual.

In [[Session 40]], the Players learn from [[Silas Seer]] that the rogue Marismente were being tracked by [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu|Captain Josias]] and the brothers at [[Fortuna Outpost]]. They were intercepted, but managed to escape after a powerful member of their group stayed behind and was captured. This member was then sent on to [[Samosetia]] for questioning at the Protectorate headquarters.

Silas says that Josias believes the rogue Marismente made their way to [[Underton]], and may be behind the faltering of coal supplies from the mine.
