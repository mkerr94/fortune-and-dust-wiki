# Overview

Shadowy association of psionic assassins also known as the Empty-Scabbard Killers.

Their symbol is that of three crossed daggers in a triangle shape.

Although they are yet known to operate in [[Alcadeña]], [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]] saw a vision of this sigil above a strange figure from her past in [[Session 31]].
