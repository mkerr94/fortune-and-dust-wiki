# Overview

Burnished copper locket found by [[Sel]] on the corpse of a Protectorate soldier in the dust-dweller’s cave during [[Session 28]] Inside is a pictogram of a young girl with black hair and light eyes.

In [[Session 31]], the locket was revealed to belong to [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint]].

The image inside the locket is that of [[Penny Flint]], the deceased younger sister of [[Telemachus ‘Mackie’ Flint|Mackie]] and [[Marius Flint|Marius]].
