# Overview

Winged messenger creatures used throughout [[Alcadeña]] to deliver notes and messages across large distances at speed.

They are described by [[Marius Flint]] during [[Session 33]] to be "round, wings, row of teeth, nippy little things."

# Description

The chiropterrings are small, spherical winged creatures. Their bodies are covered in short, stiff black fur. They have no visible eyes or ears, but have a large mouth which, when opened, spans most of their body and reveals two rows of razor sharp teeth.
