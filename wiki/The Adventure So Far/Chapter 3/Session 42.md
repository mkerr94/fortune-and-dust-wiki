# The Events of Session 42

Startled from his contemplation by [[Sel]]'s call, [[Wild Bill]] makes a run toward the horses still tied up at the abandoned camp. [[Lythara Sunstrider|Lythara]] runs inside to the bottom floor of the gatehouse and begins to assess the fortifications. Upstairs, [[Elias Blackwood|Eli]] gathers wood and nails and begins to repair the barricades to the upper windows. [[Amotti]] tries to help him by offering dirt and sand from his pockets, which Eli gratefully accepts in the interests of time.

Bill begins to untie the horses and, with the help of Ralf, leads them inside the lower floor of the gatehouse. They are visibly unsettled by the prospect of being moved inside but Bill uses his experience in equine husbandry to placate them. Realising the impending danger, Sel and [[wiki/People & Organisations/Player Characters/Caeldrid Àrmann an Gealach|Caeldrid]] lock arms and exchange magical boons, cementing their intention to defend each other with a silent locking of the eyes and nodding of the head.

Meanwhile, on the lower floor, Lythara gathers enough material to barricade the windows but cannot fortify the front door. Instead, she, Wild Bill and Ralf set about using the large logs around the campfire to shore up the entrance. Lythara uses large pitons from her supplies to secure the thick logs in place.

An unsettling silence falls upon [[The Abandoned Gatehouse]] as the Players ready themselves for whatever threat is to come. None arrives. The horses whinny restlessly as Bill attends to them. Lythara gathers enough firewood to light the hearth on the lower floor, and Amotti uses his magical dancing lights to illuminate the upper. Convinced of something left behind in the drawers upstairs, Sel investigates the unit thoroughly. At the back, he finds a hidden catch which, when pulled, reveals a false drawer in the centre. Inside, he finds an identical bottle to the one that Eli found in the locked drawer. He decides to attempt to magically identify the contents, and reveals that they are two Bottles of Sunlight. With the meagre firewood already fading in the hearth downstairs, [[Gambit]] imbues some nuts and bolts from Ralf with magical light and uses them to illuminate the bottom floor of the gatehouse.

Hours pass. Outside, the last of the sunlight dips below the horizon and casts the prairie into darkness. The Players take it in turns to keep watch, with Amotti, Caeldrid and Eli going first. Eli positions himself against the windowless south wall upstairs, and after a few hours of relative inactivity begins to find himself taken by tiredness. He manages to snap himself awake before falling asleep. Downstairs, Caeldrid positions himself in the centre of the floor, closes his eyes and attempts to re-commune with Gwynevere. After a moment of concentration, he feels the familiar spark of connection with his familiar and his head jerks back as he sees through her eyes.

Gwynevere climbs into the still night sky. The moon has already risen, and Caeldrid feels the owl's heart soar as its light hits her wings. She sails through the cool air, and surveys the ocean of prairie grass below. From on high, Gwynevere makes out shapes moving swiftly through the tall blades. Beginning as one, they split and arc off in snaking paths, moving toward the rear of the gatehouse. She swoops for a closer look, and sees the dark masses of beast-like figures moving in coordinated patterns towards the waiting Players.

Caeldrid returns to his own body and warns the others. He wakes Wild Bill, who coaxes the nervous horses nearer to the front of the house. Sel gives Caeldrid the second [[Bottle of Sunlight]] and wishes him luck before returning upstairs. There, Eli smashes through one of the wooden boards blocking an upstairs window and rests his crossbow on the remainder. Focusing his keen eyes, he spots a moving patch of grass and looses a bolt. There is a loud yelp as it finds its mark and he sees four other shapes fan out and begin to circle the gatehouse. Gambit casts a magical snare on the right bottom-floor window.

Bill lies in wait at the front door. He focuses his hearing, and a chill runs down his spine as he hears a rustling and snorting - followed by the sound of tearing flesh. Moving centimetre by centimetre, he lowers his eye to a gap in the fortifications and looks out to the abandoned camp. He sees three shapes huddled over the corpse of the [[The Protectorate|Protectorate]] soldier. Three monstrous coyotes, ragged and battle-worn. The alpha, hulking over the others, feeds on the corpse and snaps at his pack-mates when they try to steal a sliver of meat. Bill recoils, and slowly pushes the barrel of his revolver through the gap in the door. He fires two bullets, one embedding itself in the corpse with a sickening squelch and one striking the alpha coyote. It snarls, and pads toward the door. Bill, frozen in place, watches as its nostrils flare and its rancid breath condenses on his face just inches away. The coyote raises its head to the moon and howls.

The coyotes scatter, fanning out and working as one as they hide in the prairie grass and circle the gatehouse. Wild Bill, Lythara, Gambit and Caeldrid defend the bottom floor and the horses, while Eli, Amotti, Sel and Silas prepare to deal with a breach on the upper.

The coyotes make coordinated attempts to leap from the prairie grass and smash themselves into the barricades at the lower windows. One smashes through, deftly leaping straight through Gambits magical snare. The horses go wild, kicking and showing the whites of their eyes, but Bill commands them and makes sure they don't damage themselves and the party. As he does, he spins as he hears another coyote smash through the remaining bottom floor window. Lythara rushes in and cleaves off one of the animal's legs with her glaive. The creature, reckless with feeding frenzy, looks up at the horses as saliva drips from its maw. Upstairs, the others listen to the carnage unfolding downstairs and focus their weapons on the door.
