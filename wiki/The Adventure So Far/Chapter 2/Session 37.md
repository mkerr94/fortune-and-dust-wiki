# The Events of Session 37

The Players look on in horror as [[Maeve]] holds her hand aloft, gleaming with purple energy, and begins to levitate an incapacitated [[Amotti]] into the air. As the light around Maeve intensifies, [[Pastor Jeremiah|Jeremiah]]'s form begins to fade and he disappears. [[Captain Josias Bartolomeu|Josias]] rallies his men to action. The soldiers draw their weapons and rush out into the training yard, but are met by several billowing missiles of purple smoke that burst forth from Maeve. Upon landing on the ground in front of the soldiers,  giant spiders emerge from the smoke cloaked in shadow and engage Josias and his men in melee - leaving the Players to face Maeve alone.

Quick to the draw, [[Wild Bill]] fires off two shots from his twin pistols at Maeve. The impact of his bullets distracts her long enough that her grip on Amotti is released and he falls to the ground, breathing heavily as he returns to consciousness. Maeve turns her attention to the Players and their battle begins.

Maeve attacks with augmented powers of poison and decay. Moving swiftly through the air, she attempts to sink her claws, extending from her hands and made of iridescent violet energy, into the Players and sap the life from them. She uses the power granted to her by [[Lolth]] to raise a shadowy copy of herself, with burnt out hollow eye sockets smouldering with heat, to fight on her behalf. Spider-like limbs emerge from this creature's back and it covers a large portion of the battlefield in a treacherous web which ensnares the Players and hinders their movements.

[[wiki/People & Organisations/Player Characters/Caeldrid Àrmann an Gealach|Caeldrid]] retaliates. Summoning the power of his sacred moonlight to the Hollowsteel blade, he slashes at Maeve - brilliant green carving through the insidious purple. While she is engaged by Caeldrid, Amotti calls upon [[Eilistraee]] for aid and sends down a shaft of radiant moonlight on Maeve. She recoils and evaporates, however the essence left behind in her wake makes its way into her copy. As she occupies it, the hollow eyes of Maeve's copy gradually fill and eventually glow with a cold lilac flame.

The Players continue to struggle against Maeve's rising power. Wild Bill and [[Gambit]] pepper her with shots from their revolvers, while Caeldrid and Amotti try to counter her dark power with the strength of their moonlight energy. All the while, the web laid out by Maeve's copy continues to entangle the Players and leave them vulnerable to attack. The battle is vicious, with both the Players and Maeve taking grievous blows. Gambit, overcome by the power of her poison, almost succumbs but is shielded by the stoic figure of Ralf. To Maeve's anger and disgust, Ralf's steel body is immune to her poisonous attacks, and he is able to defend himself and his master from her blows while retaliating with his powerful fists. The Players begin to chip at Maeve's armour.

Gambit, fully trapped in the web and growing more and more desperate as he struggles to break free, looks up as Maeve bears down upon him - smile glinting wildly and eyes intent on death. With a last effort, he manages to free his pistol and fires two bullets into a gap in Maeve's armour. Purple energy seems to leak from the wounds and Maeve sinks to the ground.

> *“How… how? No matter. This isn't the end for us, Amotti, only the beginning. Until next time…"*

Maeve flashes a last, knowing grin at Gambit and, in a burst of dark smoke, disappears. Caeldrid speculates aloud whether this Maeve was merely a phantom, a shade of the real thing. The Players watch as the smoke as it twists like a snake on the ground of the training yard - before shooting off with a burst of speed towards the lock-up and young [[Marius Flint]] inside…
