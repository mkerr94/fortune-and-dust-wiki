# Overview

Magical staff once wielded by Brez-ak and recovered by Sel after his defeat in Session 29. It is crafted from driftwood and bears a carved face from which two eyes glowing with iridescent blue liquid peer out.  

![[staff_of_water_binding.png|500]]

Requires Attunement

Casting Time: 1 action

Charges: 6 per long rest total, recharge 1d6 per long rest (e.g. use 2, have 4 charges after rest and can recharge 1d6)  
Range: 60 feet

Components: V, S  
Duration: Instantaneous

You cause tendrils of icy blue water to form around one creature that you can see within range. The target must make a Constitution saving throw DC 16. On a failed save, the target  takes 2d6 cold damage, and it has disadvantage on the next weapon attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
