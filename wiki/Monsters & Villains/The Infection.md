# Overview

Mysterious corrupting influence that spreads through [[Fortuna Outpost]] during the Players time there.

It is characterised by glowing purple eyes, purple energy rising like steam from the hands and a desire above all else to pass this influence on to others.

In [[Session 35]], [[Wild Bill]] discovers that he is the only member of the group to remain uninfected when he is attempted to be turned by [[Amotti]]. Wild Bill hears a seductive female voice in his head as Amotti approaches.

> *"I know you, William. I see you, inside and out. Aren't you tired of this fear? Tired of this endless struggle to outrun what you've done, what you've seen? Come to me, I'm here… I offer you the sweetest, warmest oblivion… surrender yourself to it now… leave all of this behind… why fight any longer, when you could sleep now?"*

In [[Session 36]], this voice is revealed to be that of [[Maeve]]. The infection spreading throughout camp was that of her corrupting influence, her control. She has been using this to torment the already troubled soldiers of Fortuna, seemingly for her own amusement.
