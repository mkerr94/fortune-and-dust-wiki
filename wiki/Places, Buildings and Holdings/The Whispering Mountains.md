# Overview

Mountain range north of the capital city of [[Samosetia]] first mentioned to the Players by [[Silas Seer]] during [[Session 31]].

Silas describes the mountains as the principle mining source of the rare metal [[Dimeritium]], the iron alloy of which is used in various forms by [[The Protectorate]] to suppress the abilities of mage-born.
