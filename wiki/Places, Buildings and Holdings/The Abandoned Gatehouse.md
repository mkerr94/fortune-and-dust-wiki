# Overview

Outpost formerly run by [[The Protectorate]] along the disused trail between [[Fortuna Outpost]] and [[Underton]] mining town.

Found by the Players during [[Session 41]], it is in an abandoned state and shows signs of a siege and ensuing battle. It is encircled by a sea of tall prairie grass. There is a camp outside showing signs that bodies have been dragged off along the ground and into the grass. The Players find the corpse of a Protectorate soldier that has had his lower jaw ripped off by something long-fingered and incredibly strong.

Inside, the windows have been boarded up but smashed in from the outside. The scant furniture has been upturned and used to create makeshift barricades. On the rear outer wall, there are signs that some creature with large claws has scaled the building and smashed its way in to one of the windows on the upper floor, dragging a body back out of the window.

In a chest of drawers in the upper room, the Players find two Bottles of Sunlight (see [[Bottle of Sunlight]]) and a note that reads simply "Stay in the light".
