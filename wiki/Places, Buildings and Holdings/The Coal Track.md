# Overview

Stretch of railway connecting the coal mines of [[Underton]] to the city of [[Portumluna]]. Used for making shipments of coal from the mine to the harbour city and then beyond to the capital of [[Samosetia]].

Mentioned by [[Silas Seer]] during [[Session 40]] as a means by which he and the Players may make the journey to Portumluna if they can get to Underton.
