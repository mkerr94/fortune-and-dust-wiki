# Overview

Strange weather phenomenon encountered by the Players during [[Session 29]], after having travelled west from the exit of the Dust-land canyons. The Dust-storm is a huge maelstrom of sand, dust and rock that swirls endlessly in a radius spanning miles. As the Players approach, they are buffeted by grit and the sound of the wind, however find that they are able to walk into the tempest without being swept away.
