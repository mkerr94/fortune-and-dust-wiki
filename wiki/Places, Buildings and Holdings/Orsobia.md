# Overview

Island prison colony occupying the Harbour of the Moon in [[Portumluna]] bay.

The Players passed this prior to the beginning of the story on their passage from Faerun.

It is described by [[Silas Seer]] during [[Session 31 ]] as a stone fortress on a rocky outcropping, and was the place of his birth.

> *“You’d much rather be locked up in here than in there, of that I assure you.”*
