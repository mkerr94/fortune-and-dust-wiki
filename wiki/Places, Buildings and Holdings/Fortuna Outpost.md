# Overview

[[The Protectorate|Protectorate]] fort to the west of [[Clement's Hollow]].

Mentioned to the players by Holden Smith during the events of [[Session 2]].

During the Clement’s Hollow town meeting in [[Session 6]], [[Magnolia 'Old Mags' Aurix|Old Mags]] mentions that she has called for aid to Fortuna requesting soldiers to help defend the town.

The Players arrive here after passing through the dust-storm in [[Session 30]]. They find that it is a large, rectangular fort made of fortified wood and iron. Its walls house several substructures, and are lined by a perimeter walkway with watchtowers. Flags bearing the twin iconography of the Protectorate (The Watchful Sword) and the country of [[Alcadeña]] (The Founder’s Wheel) hang above the entry gate.
